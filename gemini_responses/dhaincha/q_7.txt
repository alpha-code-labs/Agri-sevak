As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I can confirm the following regarding the diseases mentioned for Dhaincha:

*   **Dieback:** This is not known to affect Dhaincha.
*   **Twig blight:** This is not known to affect Dhaincha.
*   **Tikka disease:** This is not known to affect Dhaincha. Tikka disease is a specific leaf spot disease primarily affecting groundnut (peanut).
