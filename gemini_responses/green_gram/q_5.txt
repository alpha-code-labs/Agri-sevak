As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I can confirm the following regarding the conditions mentioned for Green Gram cultivation in Haryana:

*   **Scab:** This is not known to affect Green Gram.
*   **Canker:** This is not known to affect Green Gram.
*   **Gummosis:** This is not known to affect Green Gram.
*   **Lichen:** This is not known to affect Green Gram.

Common diseases affecting Green Gram in Haryana include Mungbean Yellow Mosaic Virus (MYMV), Cercospora Leaf Spot, Powdery Mildew, and Anthracnose, for which specific management strategies are recommended. The conditions you've listed are generally not associated with Green Gram.
