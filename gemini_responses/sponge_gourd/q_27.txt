**Climate:**
Sponge Gourd is a warm-season crop that thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.
*   **Temperature:** It requires a long warm growing season. The optimum temperature range for vigorous growth, flowering, and fruiting is between 25째C to 35째C. Temperatures below 15째C or above 40째C can adversely affect growth and fruit set.
*   **Frost:** It is highly susceptible to frost, which can severely damage the plants. Therefore, cultivation should be planned to avoid frost periods.
*   **Humidity:** Moderate to high humidity is generally favorable for its growth.

**Soil Type:**
Sponge Gourd can be grown on a wide range of soils, but it performs best on specific types.
*   **Texture:** Well-drained sandy loam to loamy soils are ideal. Heavy clay soils are generally not suitable due to poor drainage and aeration.
*   **Organic Matter:** Soils rich in organic matter are highly preferred as they provide better nutrient retention and soil structure.
*   **pH:** A soil pH range of 6.0 to 7.0 (slightly acidic to neutral) is considered optimal for healthy growth and nutrient availability.

**Drainage Requirement:**
*   **Well-drained soil is crucial** for Sponge Gourd cultivation. The crop is highly sensitive to waterlogging.
*   **Excessive moisture** around the root zone can lead to root rot, fungal diseases, and impaired nutrient uptake, significantly reducing yield and plant vigor.
*   Ensure that the chosen field has good natural drainage or implement appropriate drainage measures to prevent water accumulation, especially during heavy rainfall or irrigation.
