Here are the recommended doses of organic manures and products for Capsicum cultivation in Haryana:

*   **Farm Yard Manure (FYM):**
    *   Apply 15-20 tonnes per acre during land preparation, at least 2-3 weeks before transplanting. Ensure it is well-decomposed.

*   **Vermicompost:**
    *   Apply 3-5 tonnes per acre during land preparation or incorporate it into the soil at the time of transplanting. It can also be used as a top dressing during early growth stages.

*   **Sagarika (IFFCO):**
    *   **Sagarika Granules (for soil application):** Apply 8-10 kg per acre at the time of transplanting or within 15-20 days after transplanting, near the root zone.
    *   **Sagarika Liquid (for foliar spray):** Mix 2-3 ml per litre of water.
        *   First spray: 20-25 days after transplanting.
        *   Subsequent sprays: At 15-20 day intervals, depending on crop vigour and requirement (typically 2-3 sprays).
