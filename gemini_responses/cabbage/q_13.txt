As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I must inform you that:

*   **Fruit fly** is not known to affect Cabbage. Fruit flies primarily infest fruits and some fruiting vegetables like cucurbits, tomatoes, and brinjal.
*   **Ber fly** (*Carpomyia vesuviana*) is not known to affect Cabbage. Ber fly is a specific pest of Ber (Indian Jujube) fruits.
