Based on scientific understanding and agricultural practices for wheat cultivation in Haryana:

*   **Dieback** is not known to affect Wheat. This term typically refers to the progressive death of twigs and branches in woody plants.
*   **Twig blight** is not known to affect Wheat. This term also refers to diseases affecting the twigs of woody plants.
*   **Tikka disease** is not known to affect Wheat. Tikka disease is a common leaf spot disease primarily affecting Groundnut (Peanut).
