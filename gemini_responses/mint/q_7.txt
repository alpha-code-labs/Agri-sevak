As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I can confirm the following regarding the diseases mentioned for Mint cultivation in Haryana:

*   **Dieback:** This is not known to affect Mint as a specific disease.
*   **Twig blight:** This is not known to affect Mint as a specific disease.
*   **Tikka disease:** This is not known to affect Mint. Tikka disease is primarily a disease of groundnut (peanut).
