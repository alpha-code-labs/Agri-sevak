As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I can confirm the following regarding the conditions you've inquired about for Rocket salad (Eruca sativa):

*   **Scab:** This is not known to affect Rocket salad.
*   **Canker:** This is not known to affect Rocket salad.
*   **Gummosis:** This is not known to affect Rocket salad.
*   **Lichen:** This is not known to affect Rocket salad. Lichens are symbiotic organisms that grow on surfaces like tree bark or rocks and are not parasitic to plants. They do not cause disease or damage to herbaceous crops like Rocket salad.
