Black Rust (Stem Rust), Brown Rust (Leaf Rust), Yellow Rust (Stripe Rust), White Rust, and Red Rust are specific diseases primarily affecting other crops such as cereals (e.g., wheat, barley, oats), crucifers (e.g., mustard, cabbage), or perennial plants (e.g., tea, coffee, guava).

**These specific rust diseases are not known to affect Tobacco.**
