*   Excellent soil drainage is absolutely crucial for Bottle Gourd cultivation.

*   Soils with poor drainage can suffocate the roots, impairing nutrient uptake and overall plant health.
*   In areas prone to water accumulation, cultivation on raised beds or ridges is strongly recommended to ensure proper drainage and aeration around the root zone.
