Ber fly (*Carpomyia vesuviana*) is a specific pest of Ber (Ziziphus mauritiana) and does not affect Pigeon pea.

While some generalist fruit flies (Tephritidae) can infest various fruits, they are not considered a known major pest of Pigeon pea. The primary insect pests affecting Pigeon pea pods are pod borers (e.g., *Helicoverpa armigera*, *Maruca vitrata*, *Lampides boeticus*, *Exelastis atomosa*).
