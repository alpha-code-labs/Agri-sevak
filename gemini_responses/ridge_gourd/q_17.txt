As a Senior Agronomist at HAU, Hisar, here are the recommended doses for organic manures and bio-stimulants for Ridge Gourd cultivation in Haryana:

*   **Farmyard Manure (FYM):** Apply 10-15 tonnes per acre during field preparation, thoroughly incorporating it into the soil.
*   **Vermicompost:** Apply 2-4 tonnes per acre during field preparation, thoroughly incorporating it into the soil.
*   **Sagarika (Seaweed Extract based bio-stimulant):**
    *   **Foliar Spray:** 2-3 ml per litre of water, applied at critical growth stages (e.g., vegetative growth, pre-flowering, fruit development).
    *   **Soil Application/Drenching:** 500 ml to 1 litre per acre, mixed with irrigation water or applied to the soil, especially during early growth stages.
