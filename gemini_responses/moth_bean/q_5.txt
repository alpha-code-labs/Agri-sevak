As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I can confirm the following regarding the conditions mentioned for Moth Bean (Vigna aconitifolia):

*   **Scab:** This is not known to affect Moth Bean.
*   **Canker:** This is not known to affect Moth Bean.
*   **Gummosis:** This is not known to affect Moth Bean.
*   **Lichen:** This is not known to affect Moth Bean. Lichens are symbiotic organisms that typically grow on woody perennial plants or inert surfaces and are not parasitic to herbaceous annual crops like Moth Bean.
