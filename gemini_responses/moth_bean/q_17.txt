Here are the recommended doses of Organic Manure for Moth Bean cultivation in Haryana:

*   **Farm Yard Manure (FYM):**
    *   Apply 5-10 tonnes per acre.
    *   It should be well-decomposed and incorporated into the soil 2-3 weeks before sowing.

*   **Vermicompost:**
    *   Apply 1-2 tonnes per acre.
    *   It can be applied at the time of field preparation or sowing.

*   **Sagarika (IFFCO Sagarika):**
    *   **Liquid Formulation:**
        *   For foliar spray, apply 250-500 ml per acre, diluted in 150-200 litres of water.
        *   Typically, two sprays are recommended: the first at the pre-flowering stage and the second 15-20 days after the first spray.
    *   **Granular Formulation:**
        *   Apply 8-10 kg per acre as a basal application at the time of sowing.
