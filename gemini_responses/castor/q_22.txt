Here are the recommended cultivation practices for Castor in Haryana:

*   **Sowing method/pattern:**
    *   Castor is typically sown directly in the field.
    *   Line sowing is recommended, either by drilling or dibbling, to facilitate inter-cultivation operations.
    *   It can be grown as a sole crop or as an intercrop with suitable companion crops.

*   **Planting depth:**
    *   Seeds should be planted at a depth of 3-5 cm.

*   **Spacing:**
    *   For sole cropping, maintain a spacing of:
        *   Row-to-row: 90 cm
        *   Plant-to-plant: 30 cm

*   **Seed dose:**
    *   For sole cropping, a seed rate of 5-8 kg/acre is recommended.
