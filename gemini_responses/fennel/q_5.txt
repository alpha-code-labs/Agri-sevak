As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I can confirm the following regarding the conditions mentioned for Fennel cultivation in Haryana:

*   **Scab:** This is not known to affect Fennel.
*   **Canker:** This is not known to affect Fennel.
*   **Gummosis:** This is not known to affect Fennel.
*   **Lichen:** This is not known to affect Fennel. Lichens are typically epiphytic organisms that grow on woody stems or rocks and do not parasitize herbaceous plants like Fennel.
