As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), here are the scientifically verified recommendations for the application of organic manures in Pearl Millet (Bajra) for Haryana:

*   **Farm Yard Manure (FYM):**
    *   Apply 10-15 tonnes per acre.
    *   It should be well-decomposed and incorporated into the soil at least 2-3 weeks before sowing.

*   **Vermicompost:**
    *   Apply 2-4 tonnes per acre.
    *   It can be applied at the time of field preparation or as a top dressing.

*   **Sagarika (Seaweed Extract):**
    *   **Granular (Sagarika G):** Apply 8-10 kg per acre at the time of sowing or as a basal application.
    *   **Liquid (Sagarika Z+):**
        *   **Foliar Spray:** 2.5-5 ml per litre of water (500-1000 ml per acre) at tillering stage and again at pre-flowering stage.
        *   **Seed Treatment:** 10 ml per kg of seed.
