As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I can confirm the following regarding the conditions you've mentioned for Sugarcane:

*   **Scab:** This is not known to affect Sugarcane.
*   **Canker:** This is not known to affect Sugarcane.
*   **Gummosis:** This is not known to affect Sugarcane.
*   **Lichen:** This is not known to affect Sugarcane. Lichens are typically epiphytic and do not parasitize or cause damage to sugarcane plants.
