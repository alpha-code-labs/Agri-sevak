As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I can confirm the following regarding the conditions you've mentioned for FingerMillet (Ragi):

*   **Scab:** This is not known to affect FingerMillet (Ragi).
*   **Canker:** This is not known to affect FingerMillet (Ragi).
*   **Gummosis:** This is not known to affect FingerMillet (Ragi).
*   **Lichen:** This is not known to affect FingerMillet (Ragi). Lichens are symbiotic organisms that typically grow on surfaces like tree bark or rocks and are not parasitic to annual crops like FingerMillet.
