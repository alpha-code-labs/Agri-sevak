As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I can confirm the following regarding the diseases you mentioned for FingerMillet (Ragi):

*   **Dieback:** This is not known to affect FingerMillet (Ragi). Dieback is primarily a disease affecting woody plants like trees and shrubs.
*   **Twig blight:** This is not known to affect FingerMillet (Ragi). Twig blight is also a disease typically affecting woody plants.
*   **Tikka disease:** This is not known to affect FingerMillet (Ragi). Tikka disease is a well-known foliar disease specific to Groundnut (Peanut), caused by *Cercospora arachidicola* and *Phaeoisariopsis personata*.
