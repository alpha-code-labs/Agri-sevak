As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I can confirm the following regarding the diseases mentioned for Yard Long Bean:

*   **Dieback:** This is not known to affect Yard Long Bean as a specific disease entity. While various diseases or environmental stresses can cause symptoms of plant parts dying back, "Dieback" itself is not a recognized primary disease of Yard Long Bean.
*   **Twig blight:** This is not known to affect Yard Long Bean. Yard Long Bean is a herbaceous vine and does not develop woody twigs susceptible to twig blight diseases typically found in woody plants.
*   **Tikka disease:** This is not known to affect Yard Long Bean. Tikka disease is a specific foliar disease primarily affecting groundnut (peanut).
