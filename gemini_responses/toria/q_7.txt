As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I can confirm the following regarding the diseases mentioned for Toria:

*   **Dieback:** This is not known to affect Toria as a specific disease.
*   **Twig blight:** This is not known to affect Toria as a specific disease.
*   **Tikka disease:** This is not known to affect Toria. Tikka disease is primarily associated with groundnut (peanut).
