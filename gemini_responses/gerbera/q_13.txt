As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I must inform you that:

*   **Fruit fly:** This is not known to affect Gerbera as a significant pest. Fruit flies primarily infest fruits like mango, guava, ber, and cucurbits.
*   **Ber fly (Carpomyia vesuviana):** This is not known to affect Gerbera. The Ber fly is a highly specific pest of Ber (Ziziphus mauritiana) fruit.
