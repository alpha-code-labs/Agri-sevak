As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I can confirm the following regarding the diseases mentioned for Pointed Gourd:

*   **Dieback:** This is not known to affect Pointed Gourd as a specific disease. While symptoms of wilting and drying of shoots can occur due to various other fungal or bacterial diseases, "Dieback" is not a recognized disease name for Pointed Gourd.
*   **Twig blight:** This is not known to affect Pointed Gourd. "Twig blight" typically refers to diseases affecting woody stems, whereas Pointed Gourd is a herbaceous vine.
*   **Tikka disease:** This is not known to affect Pointed Gourd. Tikka disease is primarily a disease of groundnut (peanut).
