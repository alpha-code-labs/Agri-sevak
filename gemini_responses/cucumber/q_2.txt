Based on scientific literature and common agricultural practices in Haryana, the specific rust diseases you have mentioned are not known to affect Cucumber.

*   **Black Rust (Stem Rust), Brown Rust (Leaf Rust), and Yellow Rust (Stripe Rust)** are primarily diseases of cereal crops like wheat and barley, caused by *Puccinia* species.
*   **White Rust** is caused by *Albugo* species, which are oomycetes (water molds), not true rust fungi (Basidiomycetes). While *Albugo* can affect some cucurbits, it is not classified as a "rust disease" in the mycological sense, and *Albugo* species are not a common or significant pathogen of cucumber in Haryana.
*   **Red Rust** is not a specific, recognized rust disease of cucumber.
