As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I can confirm the following:

This is not known to affect Sunflower.

The specific "Smut diseases" (Black smut, False smut, Kernel smut, and Loose smut), typically caused by fungi belonging to genera like *Ustilago*, *Tilletia*, or *Sphacelotheca*, are not known to be significant or common diseases affecting Sunflower (*Helianthus annuus*).

While sunflowers can be affected by various fungal diseases, these particular types of smuts are not recognized as major pathogens of sunflower crops in Haryana or globally.
