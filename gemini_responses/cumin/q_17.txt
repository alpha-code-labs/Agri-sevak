Here are the recommended doses of organic manures for Cumin cultivation in Haryana:

*   **Farmyard Manure (FYM):** Apply 10-15 tonnes per acre. Ensure the FYM is well-decomposed and incorporate it thoroughly into the soil 2-3 weeks before sowing.
*   **Vermicompost:** Apply 2-3 tonnes per acre. This can be applied at the time of field preparation and incorporated into the soil.
*   **Sagarika (Seaweed Extract):**
    *   **Granular formulation:** Apply 8-10 kg per acre as a basal application or broadcast before sowing.
    *   **Liquid formulation:** For foliar spray, use 2-3 ml per litre of water. Apply at critical growth stages such as branching and pre-flowering.
