As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I provide the following scientifically verified advice for controlling the mentioned pests in Cumin:

1.  **Borers:**
    This is not known to affect Cumin.

2.  **Caterpillars:**
    While specific caterpillar pests are not a primary concern for Cumin, polyphagous caterpillars can occasionally infest the crop. If observed, the following control measure can be adopted:
    *   Spray with **Lambda-cyhalothrin 5% EC** at a rate of 250 ml per acre, mixed in 200-250 litres of water.

3.  **Diamondback moth (DBM):**
    This is not known to affect Cumin.

4.  **Leaf folder:**
    This is not known to affect Cumin.
