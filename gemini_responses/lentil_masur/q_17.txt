Here are the recommended doses of Organic Manure for Lentil (Masur) in Haryana:

*   **Farmyard Manure (FYM):** Apply 5-10 tonnes per acre during field preparation, at least 2-3 weeks before sowing, and incorporate it well into the soil.
*   **Vermicompost:** Apply 1-2 tonnes per acre during field preparation, incorporating it into the soil.
*   **Sagarika (IFFCO):**
    *   **Granular:** Apply 8-10 kg per acre as a basal application or broadcast before sowing.
    *   **Liquid:** For foliar application, mix 250-500 ml of Sagarika liquid in 100-150 litres of water per acre and spray at critical growth stages (e.g., branching/flowering initiation).
