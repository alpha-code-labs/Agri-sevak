For Methi (Fenugreek) cultivation in Haryana, the recommended doses for organic manures are as follows:

*   **Farm Yard Manure (FYM):** Apply **10-15 tonnes per acre** during land preparation, at least 2-3 weeks before sowing. Ensure the FYM is well-decomposed.
*   **Vermicompost:** Apply **2-4 tonnes per acre** during land preparation, or as a top dressing.
*   **Sagarika (Liquid Seaweed Extract):**
    *   **Soil Application/Drench:** Apply **1-2 litres per acre** mixed with irrigation water or as a drench, preferably at sowing/transplanting and again at the vegetative growth stage.
    *   **Foliar Spray:** For supplementary nutrient boost, apply **2-4 ml per litre of water**, sprayed during vegetative growth.
