Regarding your query:

*   **Dieback:** This is not known to affect Barseem. Dieback is typically a condition observed in woody plants where branches or shoots die from the tip backward. Barseem is an herbaceous legume.
*   **Twig blight:** This is not known to affect Barseem. Twig blight refers to diseases affecting the twigs of woody plants. Barseem does not have twigs.
*   **Tikka disease:** This is not known to affect Barseem. Tikka disease is a specific and well-known fungal disease primarily affecting groundnut (peanut).
