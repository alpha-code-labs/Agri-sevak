For Cowpea (Vegetable) cultivation in Haryana, the recommended doses for organic manures are as follows, to be incorporated into the soil during field preparation:

*   **Farm Yard Manure (FYM):**
    *   Apply **10-15 tonnes per acre** (well-decomposed).
*   **Vermicompost:**
    *   Apply **2-4 tonnes per acre**.
*   **Sagarika (Granular):**
    *   Apply **8-10 kg per acre** (soil application, during sowing or transplanting).

These recommendations are general guidelines. For precise nutrient management, it is always advisable to conduct a soil test to determine the specific nutrient status of your field.
