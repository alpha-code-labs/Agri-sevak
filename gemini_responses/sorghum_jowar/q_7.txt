As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I can confirm the following regarding the diseases mentioned for Sorghum (Jowar):

*   **Dieback:** This is not known to affect Sorghum (Jowar). Dieback is primarily a disease of woody plants (trees and shrubs).
*   **Twig blight:** This is not known to affect Sorghum (Jowar). Twig blight typically affects woody plants.
*   **Tikka disease:** This is not known to affect Sorghum (Jowar). Tikka disease is a common and significant foliar disease of Groundnut (Peanut), caused by *Cercospora arachidicola* and *Phaeoisariopsis personata*.
