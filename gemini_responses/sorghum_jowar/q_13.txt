This is not known to affect Sorghum (Jowar).

Fruit flies (Tephritidae) are primarily pests of fruit crops, and Ber fly (Carpomyia vesuviana) is a specific pest of Ber (Ziziphus mauritiana). Neither of these are known to be pests of Sorghum (Jowar).

The major fly pest affecting Sorghum in Haryana is the Sorghum Shoot Fly (Atherigona soccata). If you are facing issues with shoot fly, please let us know, and we can provide appropriate control measures.
