*   **Fruit fly** (referring to species like *Bactrocera dorsalis* or *Bactrocera cucurbitae*) primarily infests fleshy fruits of horticultural crops and is not a known pest of Sesame.
*   **Ber fly** (*Carpomyia vesuviana*) is a highly host-specific pest of Ber (*Ziziphus mauritiana*) and does not infest Sesame.
