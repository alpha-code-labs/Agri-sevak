As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I can confirm the following regarding the conditions you've mentioned for Turmeric cultivation in Haryana:

*   **Scab:** This is not known to affect Turmeric.
*   **Canker:** This is not known to affect Turmeric.
*   **Gummosis:** This is not known to affect Turmeric.
*   **Lichen:** This is not known to affect Turmeric. Lichens are generally harmless epiphytes and do not parasitize or cause disease in turmeric plants.
