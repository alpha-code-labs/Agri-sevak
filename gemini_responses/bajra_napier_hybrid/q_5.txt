As a Senior Agronomist at Haryana Agricultural University (HAU, Hisar), I can confirm the following regarding the conditions mentioned for BAJRA NAPIER HYBRID:

*   **Scab:** This is not known to affect BAJRA NAPIER HYBRID.
*   **Canker:** This is not known to affect BAJRA NAPIER HYBRID.
*   **Gummosis:** This is not known to affect BAJRA NAPIER HYBRID.
*   **Lichen:** This is not known to affect BAJRA NAPIER HYBRID as a disease or condition requiring control. Lichens are symbiotic organisms that typically grow on surfaces and are not parasitic to plants.
