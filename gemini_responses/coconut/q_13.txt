**Fruit fly in Coconut:**
This is not known to affect Coconut as a primary or significant pest requiring specific control measures. While some generalist insect species might occasionally be observed, typical fruit flies (Tephritidae) are not recognized as major pests of coconut palms.

**Ber fly in Coconut:**
The Ber fly (*Carpomyia vesuviana*) is a highly host-specific pest of Ber (*Ziziphus mauritiana*) and related *Ziziphus* species. This is not known to affect Coconut.
